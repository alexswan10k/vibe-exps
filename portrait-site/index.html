About Elena

With over 15 years of experience in portrait painting, Elena Vasquez has established herself as a master of capturing the essence of her subjects. Her work has been featured in galleries across Europe and commissioned by celebrities, families, and corporations alike.

Elena's approach combines classical techniques with contemporary sensitivity, creating portraits that are both timeless and deeply personal.
