About Stellar Frontier

Stellar Frontier is a space trading and exploration game inspired by classics like Escape Velocity. Set in a vast, procedurally generated galaxy, players take on the role of a space captain navigating the challenges of interstellar commerce, diplomacy, and survival.

Built with cutting-edge technology, the game features realistic physics simulation, dynamic economies, and endless replayability. Whether you're a seasoned trader, a daring explorer, or a tactical combat pilot, Stellar Frontier offers something for every space enthusiast.
