Instructions:

  • Load Image: Click "Load Image" to select and load an image file from your local device
  • Paste Image: Click "Paste Image" button or simply press Ctrl+V to load an image from your clipboard
  • Draw: Select "Draw" tool, then click and drag to paint on the image
  • Crop: Select "Crop" tool, then click and drag to choose an area - crop applies automatically when you release
  • Clone Stamp: Select "Clone Stamp" tool, right-click and drag to set the offset distance, then left-click to stamp pixels from the offset location
  • Blur: Select "Blur" tool, then click and drag to blur pixels within the brush circle (brush size affects blur area)
  • Adjust: Use color picker and brush size slider for drawing
  • Rescale: Enter a percentage and click "Rescale" to change image size
  • Zoom: Use the + and - buttons or mouse wheel to zoom in/out on the canvas
  • Undo/Redo: Click "Undo" to revert the last action, "Redo" to restore an undone action
  • Save: Click "Save as PNG" to download your edited image
